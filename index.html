Our Products

Screw Conveyor

Savtech Solution provides high-performance screw conveyors designed for efficient handling of bulk materials such as powders, granules, and semi-solids. Built with precision and durability, our conveyors ensure smooth material flow and reliable operation for industries like cement, pharmaceuticals, food, and chemicals.

Belt Filter

At Savtech Solution , we supply advanced belt filters for continuous solid–liquid separation. Our systems are engineered for high-capacity filtration, enabling effective dewatering of slurries and wastewater. Trusted across chemical, pharmaceutical, and environmental industries, we deliver consistent results and long-lasting performance.

Storage Tanks

Savtech Solution manufactures robust storage tanks in Mild Steel (MS) and Stainless Steel (SS) for safe storage of chemicals, solvents, water, and raw materials. Our tanks are designed to withstand pressure, corrosion, and tough conditions, with customized solutions to meet your specific industry needs.

MS & SS Reactors With Jacket

Savtech Solution delivers jacketed reactors crafted from MS and SS to provide accurate temperature control for critical chemical processes. With advanced construction and superior performance, our reactors are trusted by industries that require reliable and consistent batch processing.

MS & SS Blender

Savtech Solution offers MS & SS blenders engineered for uniform mixing of powders, granules, and semi-solids. Our blenders guarantee high efficiency, consistency, and durability, serving industries such as pharmaceuticals, chemicals, food, and cosmetics with precision blending solutions.

MS & SS Reactor With Limpset Coil

We at Savtech Solution design MS & SS reactors with limpet coils for efficient heating and cooling of process fluids. Our reactors are widely used in chemical, food, and pharmaceutical industries, ensuring safety, precision, and durability for demanding production processes.

Water Treatment Plant
